Event Roadmap

Technophilia: March 2021

It is CSI’s newsletter that comes out towards the end of the odd semester. The theme for 2020-21 was " Tech in shining Armour"

Cozine: July 2021

It is our annual magazine that gets released at the end of the even semester. For 2020-21, the theme was Wayfinder to a Utopian World.

Technopedia

It is a competition where people across the college can participate. When we did it last year the prompt was “Realistic Predictions for Technology in 2040.

OpenCode: Jan 2022

OpenCode RAIT is a month long Open Source program during winters conducted by CSI RAIT, with an aim to help beginners get started with Open Source Development while encouraging diversity. Register now!
